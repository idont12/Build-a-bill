About

Hello boys and girls and welcome to the Build a bill website. This site was created by the most bored and strange student on earth who goes by the name Yardan Zilberman. In the last weeks of the summer of 2022, when everyone is freaking out at home as an act of extreme stupidity, the website began to be built as an attempt to draw something using only CSS and little by little it grew and expanded until it reached its current form…

This is a fan project based on the AMAZING tv show Gravity Falls. The inspiration came from the first appearance of bill in the show (s1 e19).
All the rights to the show original character design and gravity falls in general belong to Disney.

Thank you so much for reading and hope you enjoy the website.
